After-sales support

Export after-sales workflow from first contact to case closure.

Built for overseas dealers, importers, and fleet buyers who need a clear path for vehicle issues after shipment.

Case flow

A practical service path for exported vehicles.

Each case starts with buyer contact and is routed to technical diagnosis, parts supply, warranty coordination, or commercial settlement according to the order agreement.

Step 01 / Intake

Customer contact

Buyer contacts V-Star by WhatsApp, phone, or email and provides order number, vehicle model, VIN, location, and issue summary.

Buyer sendsOrder number, model, VIN, location, issue summary
OutputInitial case record and responsible contact

Step 02 / Registration

Case registration

V-Star records the case and checks order status, warranty notes, shipment terms, responsible contacts, and destination market details.

V-Star checksContract file, invoice, shipment record, warranty note
OutputCase route and response priority

Step 03 / Evidence

Evidence collection

Buyer submits photos, videos, diagnostic codes, mileage, maintenance records, and workshop comments before disassembly when possible.

Required proofPhotos, video, fault code, mileage, local workshop notes
OutputComplete evidence pack for supplier or warranty review

Step 04 / Diagnosis

Technical triage

The case is classified as operation guidance, consumable part replacement, shipping damage, warranty assessment, or non-warranty repair support.

Decision routeGuidance, parts, warranty, supplier feedback, or local repair
OutputRecommended handling path

Step 05 / Approval

Solution confirmation

V-Star confirms the support route with the buyer before repair cost, replacement parts, or supplier feedback work continues.

Buyer confirmsPreferred route, cost responsibility, timing, destination contact
OutputApproved service action

Step 06 / Supply

Parts and logistics

For approved parts, V-Star checks inventory, price, package weight, freight option, export documents, and dispatch timing.

V-Star preparesStock check, quotation, packing data, freight reference
OutputParts dispatch plan and logistics update

Step 07 / Follow-up

Repair follow-up

Buyer reports installation result, repair photos, test result, and any remaining symptoms so the case can be adjusted if needed.

Buyer returnsInstallation photos, test drive result, remaining symptoms
OutputConfirmed repair result or adjusted plan

Step 08 / Closure

Case closure

V-Star records the final result, keeps evidence for future claims, and updates product or supplier feedback when repeated issues appear.

Record keptFinal cause, solution, evidence, supplier feedback
OutputClosed case archive and repeat-issue note

Buyer evidence checklist

Complete evidence makes export after-sales faster.

A

Order proof

Contract, invoice, delivery note, VIN, vehicle model, trim, and destination country.

B

Issue proof

Clear photos, video, dashboard warning, diagnostic report, and mileage at issue discovery.

C

Repair proof

Local workshop inspection comments, replaced part photos, maintenance records, and test result.

D

Commercial proof

Freight damage records, customs notes, and cost estimate if compensation or part shipment is requested.
